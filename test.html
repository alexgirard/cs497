Hi! Welcome to our CS 497 project.

Tech has the power to influence our decisions and actions, whether we are aware of it or not. In our day to day lives, we interface with technology.

In this simulation, you’ll be guided through a choose-your-own-adventure story as three major roles on a team – a machine learning engineer, a UX researcher, and a product manager.

Through each role and the decisions you make, you’ll see how your choices have ethical implications.
